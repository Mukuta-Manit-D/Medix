ABOUT US

About Medix - Empowering Medical Diagnosis with AI

Welcome to Medix, an innovative platform that combines the power of deep learning with advanced medical imaging technology to assist doctors and medical professionals in diagnosing lung-related abnormalities and brain tumors. Our mission is to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of medical diagnosis, enabling early detection and timely treatment of various medical conditions.

Our Vision

At Medix, we envision a future where cutting-edge technology and medical expertise come together to revolutionize healthcare. By harnessing the potential of artificial intelligence and machine learning, we aim to empower medical professionals with an intelligent tool that aids in the early detection of lung diseases and brain tumors, ultimately leading to better patient outcomes and improved quality of life.

How Medix Works:

Medix employs state-of-the-art deep learning models that have been meticulously trained on an extensive dataset of X-ray and CT scan images. These models are capable of classifying images into 18 different categories, covering a wide range of lung-related abnormalities and brain tumors. With the click of a button, medical practitioners can upload an image, and our advanced AI system will swiftly analyze it, providing an accurate prediction of the observed abnormality.

The Model is capable of Classifying the Images into 18 Different classes Namely

  • X-001: Atelectasis
  • X-002: Brain_Tumor
  • X-003: Cardiomegaly
  • X-004: Consolidation
  • X-005: Edema
  • X-006: Effusion
  • X-007: Emphysema
  • X-008: Fibrosis
  • X-009: Hernia
  • X-010: Infiltration
  • X-011: Mass
  • X-012: No_Brain_Finding
  • X-013: No_Lung_Finding
  • X-014: Nodule
  • X-015: Pleural
  • X-016: Pneumonia
  • X-017: Pneumothorax
  • X-018: Tuberculosis
